## Growth Content Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (first 2 weeks)
- Post consistently (1–2 posts/day)
- Focus on useful, practical content
- No controversial topics
- Build trust

### Phase 2: Engagement (weeks 3–6)
- Increase to 2–3 posts/day
- Introduce engagement questions
- Encourage comments and discussion
- Start outreach to other groups

### Phase 3: Authority (weeks 7+)
- Worker reviews become the main draw
- People share experiences organically
- Group becomes go-to resource for seasonal workers
- Scale outreach carefully
